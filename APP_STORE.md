### Description
SpeakSmart turns your voice into polished, professional text. Just speak naturally and let AI transform your words into the perfect tone and format.

**How it works:**
1. Tap the microphone and start speaking
2. Review your transcription
3. Choose your tone (Professional, Casual, Funny, Polite, Concise, or Detailed)
4. Select your format (Email, Notes, Message, Memo, or Social Post)
5. Let AI rewrite your text instantly
6. Share anywhere or save for later

**Features:**
• Real-time speech-to-text transcription
• 6 AI-powered tones to match any situation
• 5 output formats for any platform
• Save and organize your recordings
• One-tap sharing to any app
• Private and secure — your API key stays on your device

**Perfect for:**
• Drafting emails on the go
• Taking meeting notes
• Creating social media posts
• Writing memos and messages
• Capturing ideas before they slip away

SpeakSmart requires an OpenAI API key to enable AI rewriting. Your key is stored securely on your device and never shared.
